Services

A Clean Home

We begin by giving your home a detailed cleaning, then followed by a system that maintains a detail-level and perfection of clean throughout your home weekly or bi- weekly, depending on your needs. Our services are thorough, consistent and customized. We also provide all the equipment and cleaning supplies.

Aromatherapy Cleaning

Instead of using conventional cleaners formulated with harsh chemicals, we use essential oils and non-toxic products. Pure essentials oils have antibacterial, anti-fungal, germicidal, antiseptic, deodorizing and even healing properties.

Kitchen

Outside, top and front of range cleaned. Drip pans or glass top surfaces wiped. Sinks cleaned and chrome shined. Fronts of all appliances cleaned. Microwave wiped inside and out. Window sills, ledges, and blinds dusted. Cobwebs removed. Doors and door frames spot cleaned. General dusting clean and sanitize counter tops and back splash. Floors vacuumed and damp mopped.

Bathrooms

Clean, scrub and sanitize showers, shower doors, bathtubs, back splash, sinks and toilets. Clean and sanitize vanities. Clean mirrors. Polish chrome. Window sills, ledges, and blinds dusted. Cobwebs removed. Doors and door frames spot cleaned, general dusting. Wash tile walls. Deodorize. Floors vacuumed and damp mopped.

Sleeping and Living Areas

Dust picture frames, knickknacks, ceiling fans, lamps, furniture, woodwork, shelves and baseboards. Remove cobwebs. Vacuum carpets. Wood floors vacuumed and damp mopped. Stairs vacuumed, empty closet floors vacuumed. Vacuum coaches, including under any cushions. Empty and clean ashtrays and wastebaskets. Changing of bed linen.

Vacation Homes

I provide a scheduled maintenance and cleaning program for your vacation home to assure that your property stays in top condition year around.

Pricing

The price to clean your home is based on many factors. The size of your home and the frequency for cleaning are some of those factors. I will provide you a price that meets your needs, budget and cleaning requirements.
